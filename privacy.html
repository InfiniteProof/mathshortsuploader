Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MathShortsUploader ("Platform", "we", "us") handles information in connection with your use of the Platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling data transparently.

1. Information We Collect

MathShortsUploader does not collect, store, or transmit personal data from users or website visitors to any external server operated by us. Specifically:

  • We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising scripts on this website.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other identifying information through this website.
  • We do not log or store information about website visitors.

2. Social Media Account Access

The Platform connects to social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) exclusively through each platform's official OAuth authentication system. The following applies to all account access:

  • The Platform only accesses accounts that the user has personally authorized via official OAuth flows.
  • No data belonging to other users or third-party accounts is accessed, processed, or stored.
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on the user's own device and are never transmitted to or stored on MathShortsUploader servers.
  • Access is limited strictly to publishing video content to the user's own authorized accounts.

3. TikTok API Data

When using the TikTok Content Posting API, the Platform accesses only the permissions required to upload and publish video content on behalf of the authorized user. We do not access TikTok user profile data, follower information, messages, or any data beyond what is required for video publishing. Data returned by the TikTok API is used only to confirm successful upload and is not stored beyond the current session.

4. Video Content

Videos are generated and rendered locally on the user's own device. No video content is uploaded to or stored on MathShortsUploader servers. Video files are published directly from the user's device to the respective social media platform APIs.

5. Third-Party Platforms

When you connect social media accounts, you are also subject to the privacy policies of those platforms. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram independently. MathShortsUploader is not responsible for the data practices of these third-party platforms.

6. Data Security

We are committed to keeping your credentials secure. API tokens and access credentials are stored only on the user's local device. Users are responsible for keeping their devices and credentials secure. If you believe your credentials have been compromised, you should revoke API access immediately through each platform's developer or security settings.

7. Children's Privacy

MathShortsUploader is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
