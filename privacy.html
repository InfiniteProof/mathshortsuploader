Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TikTok Uploader ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. TikTok Uploader is a private tool operated by a single developer for their own TikTok account. It is not distributed or made available to anyone else.

1. Information We Collect From Website Visitors

This website does not collect, store, or transmit personal data from visitors.

  • No cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising scripts are used on this website.
  • No names, email addresses, or other identifying information are collected through this website.
  • No information about website visitors is logged or stored.

2. TikTok Account Access (Login Kit)

The app uses TikTok Login Kit so its operator can authorize their own TikTok account through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow.

  • The app connects to exactly one TikTok account: the one personally authorized by its operator through TikTok's official login flow.
  • No other TikTok account, and no data belonging to any other TikTok user, is accessed, processed, or stored.
  • The user.info.basic scope is used only to confirm the identity of the authorized account after login.
  • Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's own device and are never sent to or stored on any external server.

3. TikTok Content Posting API

The app uses the video.publish scope to post videos directly (Direct Post) to the authorized account. Before each post, the app requests the account's current posting permissions (which privacy levels are available, and whether comments, duets, and stitches are enabled) and presents these as choices the operator must set manually; nothing is selected automatically. The app does not access TikTok follower information, messages, or any data beyond what is required to complete a post. Data returned by the TikTok API is used only to confirm whether a post succeeded and is not stored beyond the current session.

4. Video Content

Videos are stored locally on the operator's own device before posting. No video content is uploaded to or stored on any external server operated by this app. Videos are sent directly from the operator's device to TikTok's API.

5. TikTok's Own Policies

Use of TikTok through this app is also subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. This app is not responsible for TikTok's data practices.

6. Data Security

API tokens and access credentials are stored only on the operator's local device. If credentials are believed to be compromised, access should be revoked immediately through TikTok's developer or account security settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
